# DreamTome — UI/UX Style Manifesto
DreamTome embraces skeuomorphism. Everything should feel like a real object touched by candlelight.

## Color Palette
### Parchment
- Gradient: #f9e6c8 → #e8d4b0  
- Border: #D4AF37  
- Ink: #2A1B0F, #5A4326

### Lighting
- Candle glow: warm, soft  
- Shadows: diffuse, low contrast

## Typography
### Titles
- Cormorant Unicase / Cinzel Decorative  
- Gold or parchment-dark  
- Uppercase or small caps

### Body
- Spectral (serif)  
- Gentle line-height  
- No pure black

## Layout Philosophy
- Objects appear layered  
- Soft shadows  
- Slight perspective distortion  
- Scroll areas hidden until needed

## Motion Guidelines
- Ease-in-out curves  
- Flip: 0.6–0.7s  
- Hover: 120–150ms  
- Press: slight sink

## Component Rules
- Preserve 3D transform chain  
- Backface visibility must stay hidden  
- Isolate overflow clipping

## Forbidden
- Neon colors  
- Sans-serif body fonts  
- Harsh edges  
- Flat UI aesthetics
